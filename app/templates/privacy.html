This is a placeholder. The final privacy policy will be published before public launch.

Cartarch stores the data you provide to manage your collection — card inventory, deck lists, storage locations, game history — and keeps it scoped to your account. Account credentials (email, password hash, optional display name) are used only for signing in and recovering access. No telemetry is collected; no third- party analytics are loaded.

Outbound network calls from Cartarch are limited to two services: Scryfall (card data lookups, executed off the request path via a local mirror) and Resend (the transactional-email provider for password resets). Card images render from the local mirror; no third-party trackers are embedded.

Questions: support@cartarch.com.
