Add Cards

Bring cards in from a CSV export, paste a list, or look up printings by name. Cartarch previews every change before it commits — nothing lands in your collection without your sign-off.

§ Upload CSV

Supported formats: Helvault (free & pro), Moxfield collection export, and the built-in Scanner App format. The format is detected automatically from the column headers.

§ Paste Card List

Paste a deck or collection list from Moxfield, MTGA, MTGO, or any tool that exports in N Card Name (SET) Collector# format. Lines with a quantity and just a name also work — e.g. 4 Lightning Bolt or 1 Mizzix of the Izmagnus — resolving to the default printing; add a (SET) to pin a specific one. You can also paste bare SET Collector# lines (with optional quantity) to add cards by set + collector number alone — e.g. MH3 145, MH3 145 2, or 2 MH3 145. Append *F* for foil, or a 2–3 letter language code like *JP* / *DE* / *FR* / *IT* / *ES* / *PT* / *RU* / *KO* / *ZHS* / *ZHT* for non-English printings. Combine markers, e.g. WAR 171 *JP* or 1 Sol Ring (LTC) 284 *F* *DE*.

§ Search by Name

Look up printings on Scryfall and pick the one you have in hand.

§ Manual Entry

Enter a Scryfall ID, or an exact set code and collector number.
