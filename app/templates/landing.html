The ruler of your collection

Cartarch is a premium collectible-management platform built for the physical workflows of serious Magic: the Gathering collectors — track ownership across boxes, binders, and built decks; analyze deck shape and consistency; run games at the table.

Built for the physical workflow

Collection tracking

Import from a barcode scanner, Helvault, Moxfield, or a pasted decklist — Cartarch normalizes the format and resolves every card against a local Scryfall mirror. Search with full Scryfall-style syntax (boolean operators, color identity, format legality, price filters) across your whole collection.

The drawer sorter

An opt-in physical-layout system that assigns every card a drawer and slot deterministically, so a re-sort always lands at the same place. Tuned for a 6-drawer setup; configurable per-location modes let any storage layout opt in or out of the auto-sort.

Deck analytics

Every deck gets a health panel: ramp, draw, removal, and wipe density against tunable thresholds; pip strain across demand vs. land sources; mana curve, card-type breakdown, color pips, avg CMC. Filter by role tags (Ramp / Draw / Removal / Combo piece / Payoff / Protection) for surgical tuning.

The game tracker

A landscape tablet-first tracker for the at-table game. Commander art panel backgrounds (loaded from the local Scryfall mirror), per-seat life + counters, turn timing, auto-elimination on lethal poison, history + undo. First- player selection by dice roll or manual tap.
